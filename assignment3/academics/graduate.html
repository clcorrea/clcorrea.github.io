Graduate Studies

Our master's and advanced certificate of graduate studies programs offer an affordable, flexible way to enhance your professional credentials and advance your career. At Northern Vermont University, we offer interdisciplinary and professional programs on-campus and online. Our faculty are experts in their fields with professional experience to help you find your direction. You will benefit from our small courses and cohorts and develop a strong, supportive peer network that will last well beyond your time at NVU.
